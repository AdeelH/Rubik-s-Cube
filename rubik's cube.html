Click the button (or press shift+c) to toggle camera controls.
When on, left-click-and-drag to move camera, right-click-and-drag to pan, and scroll to zoom.
 Rotate on x-axis: up/down arrow (ctrl+up/down for all)
Rotate on y-axis: left/right arrow (ctrl+left/right for all)
Rotate on z-axis: shift+left/right arrow (ctrl+shift+left/right for all)
